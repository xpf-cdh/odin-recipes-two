Veggie Thali Recipe

Northern Indian Style Vegetarian Thali

Thali (meaning "plate" or "tray") or Bhojanam (meaning "full meal") is a round platter used to serve food in South Asia, Southeast Asia and the Caribbean. Thali is also used to refer to an Indian-style meal made up of a selection of various dishes which are served on a platter. Thali is also used in south Asia for ceremonial purposes.

Every thali will have different dishes because we do have a wide variety of Indian dishes. Also, you will find that a north Indian thali is very different from a south Indian thali. And almost every Indian state has its own local dishes that make up its version of the thali. So, no one can tell you that a thali must have a certain dish.

For this thali, I have chosen our family favorites and the recipe for those is below in the recipe card.

Ingredients

  • Mixed Veggie Curry
  • Chana Masala
  • Dal
  • Basmati Rice
  • Chapati

How to make Veggie Thali

  1. Make a mixed veggie curry
  2. Make a chana masals
  3. Make a dal
  4. Make some basmati rice
  5. Make some chapati
  6. Arrange all the components on a single platter. The rice should be in the middle and all the thalis should be surrounding the rice
  7. Serve with some onion and relish garnish and traditional yougurt

Enjoy!!
